10 Business You Can Start Right Away

byJosh DarlingSept 18th, 2019

Today’s world presents a lot of opportunities as populations are increasing all over the world, and human’s taste keeps changing. This era has brought some of the most innovative ways to do stuff and an increase in sophisticated goods and services and yet a simple way of making money..

In this article, we'll explore some of the ways to make some money and be financially independent.

Driving

These days, driving is one of the easiest businesses you can start ,especially with the introduction of ride sharing apps. Uber and Lyft are great tools you can start with. You can either drive your own car, or drive someone’s car. Find out more about Uber or Lift from Google.

Tutoring

Another business you can start right away with little or no cost, is tutoring. The internet has made it possible for almost everything to be done online. Teaching has now gone online too, with many people teaching all sorts of skills in virtual environments. You can teach English as a TEFL Teacher or teach coding on Udemy, or teach whatever you want. Just google around and find sites where you can teach in a virtual environment. You could also teach over Skype if you already have your students.

Shopify

Shopify is an online commerce platform that allows you to set up a virtual store in just a few minutes. The beauty of Shopify is, you can still make some money even if you don’t have any products to sell. This is so because Shopify also has a drop-shipping feature you can take advantage of and make money. With drop-shipping, you sell goods to customers directly to customers from suppliers or manufacturers.

Resume Writer

If you’re good at writing and profiling people, you could write resumes and CVs for potential employees or students to land them a chance at whatever they’re applying for. This could fetch you some real cash as people are already paying to use software to write their resumes.

Airbnb

So this one is an app that pairs travelers up with hosts for an available room at a fee. Assuming your kids are away at boarding school, you can cash in on that idle room. Better still you can dedicate one room in your house to this effect, furnishing it and make it look standard for a paying guest to enjoy their stay in.

Web Development

Web design is one of the hottest jobs now. You can check the stats on google. The influx of tech has caused a huge demand for online presence, both for individuals and for organizations too. If you have the skills and know-how, you can cash in on this new trend. All you need is to know some HTML,CSS & JAVASCRIPT. These are the basics that will get you going. There are other softwares out there that can make web designing easy for you, like Bootstrap Studio, Adobe Dreamweaver, Wix, Wordpress, etc. To start you web design journey with no knowledge of coding, you can take a look at w3schools.com , freecodecamp.org ,codecademy.com .

App Development

Just like web design, App development also brings in some very good cash. Edx.org , Udacity have some very good courses on app development. Apps are the next big thing now, gradually taking over websites. A career in App development will surely save your future.

Social Media Consultant

If you are a social media person, this would be very fitting for you. You can help organizations establish their presence on various social media platforms or you could just advertise for organizations on social media. Again you could provide companies with trends online to better enhance their product quality or service delivery.

Blogging/Vlogging

Blogging has been around for a while now, so people are already aware of this. Vlogging on the hand is quite new, but its all in the same line. Blogging deals with getting articles online about something or someone, usually a niche you can write so well about, like entertainment, fashion, tech, etc. Vlogging is also about making videos on subject matters just like the above listed. You could just film your travels or even your daily live. To make money from this, you would need to pull in some huge traffic to whatever platform you are using so you could advertise to people coming on that site and earn commissions from these adverts. Usually, most people use YouTube as it is easier to monetize your content there.

Graphic Design

Creative minds can benefit a lot from this business. Once you can put someone’s creative idea or imagination into an artistic piece, you’re sure to make some real cash here. It could be flyers, banners, posters, etc, or logos. Just be able to design something catchy and earn on that.
