10 Business You Can Start Right Away

byLionel IssomboSept 18th, 2019

A friend once said "if you are cutting a tree with an axe between your bare hands, you will always end up having blisters before you finish the job"...

Years ago when I was 14, I wanted to become a soldier, a basketball professional player and a professional wrestler, all at the same time. Unfortunately, my parents opposed to all these ideas, claiming that "you are not gonna do useless things!". However, most parents, if not all, want a bright future for their kids. "Well, but you do not give me any idea", I said internally. Upset, I decided to rebel against them. I dropped out from high school for 2 years, lying to my parents that I subscribed for evening class sessions. Not limited to that, I decided to find few jobs so as to make money..

I liked spending time at the gym during that period, because it helped me dream and feel strong. But it was a short dream, reality bounced back and my parents discovered that I dropped out for 2 years. I was always waiting for a solution to appear by magic, neither giving any orientation to my life nor even thinking about the future. The day I came back from my random occupation, my father organized a family meeting. He barely looked at me, ashamed, and asked "what do you want to do with your life?"and I said "I don't Know". Then, my mother cried

I never saw my mother crying for me before. It was as if I received a severe electroshock... it was horribly painful. I could not even cry when I realized what I had done . Since then, I decided to do all I can to never make her cry again, but smile forever and paying for all the bad things that I have done. Of course, it was a challenge to find my way... but this time I was determine to succeed.

Afterwards, I engaged in a deep introspection to define what I liked; and I found out that I liked making new friends, making people smile and solving their problems by giving them advice. I finally had a passion! To further understand what career I really wanted to follow, I returned to school and successfully completed high school education, but I never gave up on my favorite sports.

Resume Writer

If you’re good at writing and profiling people, you could write resumes and CVs for potential employees or students to land them a chance at whatever they’re applying for. This could fetch you some real cash as people are already paying to use software to write their resumes.

However, during the application period for undergraduate programs, I had a sever injury: complete rupture of thigh-knee tendon. I had a surgery. The recovery was really painful, but the pain was a mixture of physical and mental stresses. Still on my hospital bed, I received a news that my application for the faculty of Political Science and Public Administration failed... I was even more depressed; my only chance to succeed vanished... I did not want to live anymore.

After a week of reflection, I understood that I have only two choice in this life: succeeding or die trying. I swore to myself that I will never ever let my passion fade away, no matter what happens. From that day, my life changed! I decided to take every challenge, big or small; nourishing my heart with passion and determination... I made pain and hard times my friends forever.

App Development

Just like web design, App development also brings in some very good cash. Edx.org , Udacity have some very good courses on app development. Apps are the next big thing now, gradually taking over websites. A career in App development will surely save your future.

Social Media Consultant

If you are a social media person, this would be very fitting for you. You can help organizations establish their presence on various social media platforms or you could just advertise for organizations on social media. Again you could provide companies with trends online to better enhance their product quality or service delivery.

Blogging/Vlogging

Blogging has been around for a while now, so people are already aware of this. Vlogging on the hand is quite new, but its all in the same line. Blogging deals with getting articles online about something or someone, usually a niche you can write so well about, like entertainment, fashion, tech, etc. Vlogging is also about making videos on subject matters just like the above listed. You could just film your travels or even your daily live. To make money from this, you would need to pull in some huge traffic to whatever platform you are using so you could advertise to people coming on that site and earn commissions from these adverts. Usually, most people use YouTube as it is easier to monetize your content there.

Graphic Design

Creative minds can benefit a lot from this business. Once you can put someone’s creative idea or imagination into an artistic piece, you’re sure to make some real cash here. It could be flyers, banners, posters, etc, or logos. Just be able to design something catchy and earn on that.
